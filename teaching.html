Teaching and Private Lessons

Teaching:

I am currently teaching at the Toronto School of Art. Sign up for a course!

Private Lessons or Portfolio Review:

I am available for private instruction or portfolio review. I have experience teaching all ages from in high school age to seniors.

Take your drawing or painting to the next level by learning:

  • Light and colour theory
  • The principles of observation and measuring
  • Seeing in terms of shape
  • The process of painting using the “block in” method then refining with details.
  • Why palette set-up is so important to right thinking when painting.
  • The rules of composition on a two-dimensional surface and why it’s so important in making a lasting work of art.
  • How to make your own canvases
  • Examples of from art history will be discussed as well as critiques of your own projects.

Cost:

  • $75/hr for a one time review.
  • $60/hr for regularly scheduled lessons (typically 1 to 1.5 hours per session).
  • Driving distance is a factor and will be added to total cost.
